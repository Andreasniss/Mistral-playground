# Mistrag HR Policy - Employee Benefits

## Vacation and Leave Policy

### Annual Leave Entitlement
- **Full-time employees**: 25 paid vacation days per calendar year
- **Part-time employees**: Pro-rated based on hours worked
- **New employees**: Accrue vacation at a rate of 2.08 days per month

### Public Holidays
Mistrag observes the following 10 public holidays per year:
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Good Friday
- Easter Monday
- Labor Day (May 1)
- Christmas Day (December 25)
- Boxing Day (December 26)
- Plus 4 additional local holidays based on office location

### Sick Leave
- 10 paid sick days per year
- Additional unpaid leave available with medical certificate

### Parental Leave
- 16 weeks paid maternity leave
- 4 weeks paid paternity/partner leave
- Up to 12 months unpaid parental leave

## Working Hours
- Standard work week: 40 hours (Monday-Friday)
- Core hours: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Flexible start/end times within 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM range

## Remote Work Policy
- Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Full remote work requires manager approval
- Equipment stipend: €500/year for home office setup

## Professional Development
- Annual training budget: €2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: Up to 2 conferences per year
- Tuition reimbursement: 80% for approved courses

## Compensation and Benefits
- Annual bonus: Up to 15% of base salary (performance-based)
- Stock options: Available after 1 year of employment
- Health insurance: Comprehensive coverage for employee and family
- Pension plan: 5% employer contribution

## Company Policies
- Dress code: Business casual
- Expense reporting: Monthly deadline on the 5th of each month
- Travel policy: Economy class for domestic, business class for international >6 hours
